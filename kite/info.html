Make your flight a breeze with these useful tips:
Prepare a wide, open area with a smooth surface before starting.
Keep your fingers relaxed, elbows close, and hands in front of your chest.
Hold the line gently, avoiding excessive gripping or rapid clicks.
Maintain focus upward during flight, not on your hand.
Check the connection if your kite doesn't respond to your commands.
Be mindful of changing weather conditions while browsing in the cloud.
